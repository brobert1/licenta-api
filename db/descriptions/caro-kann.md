In just over 150 variations, this Caro-Kann repertoire packs a punch.

It's light on trainable variations by design - with a specific focus on lines that transpose into one another, the game plans mesh together seamlessly - making learning a breeze.

This no-frills, lean and mean course takes a deep dive into the perspective of an improving player- focusing on low memorization with easy-to-grasp ideas. But the solidity of the lines makes it a repertoire you can keep all the way up till you reach International Master, and beyond.

With this course, you will learn the most important things that you have to focus on when you move the pieces. Such as:

- How to identify unprotected pieces;
- Take advantage of pieces exchanges;
- Achieve checkmate - the ultimate goal of the game;
- Tactics involving pawns;
- Tactics involving knights;
- How to improve your defensive abilities.

The course concludes with 64 tests to practice what you have learned and then a chapter on Difficult Exercises containing 14 tough puzzles.

Can you complete the course and ace those difficult puzzles? Manage that and you'll be competition-ready!
